My First looks on Julia

Sohan

You ever heard of Julia? No, not your neighbor, but the language Julia. Created in 2009, it recently started gaining popularity in the ML and Data Science field. Granted, it was built to be as fast as C++ and as friendly as python. A tool for the scientific community. It was destined! 


    function add(a, b)
        return a+b
    end

    print(add(1, 2))

With the above snippet, you can see the syntax is quiet similar to python's syntax and I'm neither happy nor sad. Although, I would love to see some good old C++ syntax!

It's speed is something to be reckoned with! I mean, look at this benchmark test done with Julia
It's really built for mathematical speed. It's also general-purpose, it's got tons of builtin libraires for almost anything, and the community is developing even more modules and libraires to back Julia up. And, if you want to get started in ML with Julia, take a look at Flux.jl, it's amazing!
